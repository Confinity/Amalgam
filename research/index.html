How we research

A short method we use to keep research practical, current, and decision-ready.

  1. Step 1

    Track high-signal research

    Focus on findings that affect real product and operating decisions.

  2. Step 2

    Compare with live delivery work

    Test where findings hold up in active teams and systems.

  3. Step 3

    Translate into next moves

    Turn evidence into practical recommendations teams can apply now.

  4. Step 4

    Update when evidence shifts

    Revise guidance quickly and call out what changed.
