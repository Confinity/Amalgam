Overview

Modernize or Rebuild

Use this when you are deciding between rebuilding a system and improving what you already have.

Most teams make this call late, or make it based on gut feel.

This gives you a practical way to weigh trade-offs and choose a path you are confident in.

When to use

  • Systems feel fragile or slow
  • Engineering effort is increasing
  • There's ongoing debate about rebuilding
  • Architecture is blocking progress

What's inside

  • how to assess system health clearly
  • how to compare rebuild vs improve
  • how to evaluate risk and cost
  • how to align the team on the decision
  • how to move forward in phases
